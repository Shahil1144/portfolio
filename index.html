About Me

I'm a passionate mechanical engineer from Rolpa, Nepal, with a focus on alternative fuels, renewable energy, and engine combustion. With experience at the Nepal Academy of Science and Technology and a strong academic background from Tribhuvan University, I specialize in biodiesel research, fuel characterization, and engine performance testing. My skills include AutoCAD, SolidWorks, MATLAB, and ANSYS Fluent, complemented by leadership and project management abilities. When not innovating, I enjoy exploring sustainable solutions for a greener future.
